AI Enablement Intern — Job Description
========================================

Company: Travel Corporation India (TCI)
Department: Office of the President and Country Head
Location: Gurugram, India
Type: Internship (2-3 months, possibility of extension)
Stipend: INR 25,000 per month

About TCI:
Travel Corporation India (TCI), a subsidiary of the Thomas Cook Group, is India's leading Inbound Destination Management Company with 63+ years of excellence. TCI has 500+ travel professionals across 28 offices.

Role Overview:
We are seeking curious, hands-on, and technically exceptional AI Enablement Interns to support the integration of AI capabilities into existing in-house software systems.

Required Qualifications:
- Currently pursuing B.Tech (AI/ML specialization) or M.Tech (AI/ML)
- Excellent, up-to-date knowledge of modern AI tools, frameworks, and technology stacks
- Familiarity with LLMs and generative AI platforms: OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, or open-source alternatives
- Hands-on experience with AI/ML frameworks: LangChain, LlamaIndex, Hugging Face Transformers
- Knowledge of vector databases: Pinecone, Weaviate, FAISS
- Understanding of RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architectures
- Proficiency in Python (preferred), JavaScript, Java, or C#
- Understanding of REST APIs, software integration, and application workflows
- Familiarity with databases, data structures, and cloud infrastructure (AWS/Azure/GCP)
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- High ownership, intellectual curiosity, proactive attitude
- Ability to work in fast-paced, collaborative environments
